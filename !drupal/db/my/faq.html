What is a Platinum membership?

It is completely FREE to add your profile and search our members. Only when you want to get in touch with other members, by sending, reading and replying to messages, you can purchase one of the Platinum membership packages. You can view Platinum membership costs options here.

What benefits can I enjoy as a Platinum member?

You can upgrade your account to enjoy Platinum membership benefits. You will be able to read and send messages, chat and video-chat with other members, upload up to 10 pictures

How do I delete my Free member profile?

To delete your free member profile click here. If you have any questions concerning membership cancellation and profile removal please contact us at support@novaface.com

How do I cancel my Platinum Membership?

To cancel your Platinum Membership click here. Please note by clicking the link you will send an automatic cancellation request to the Billing Department. If you have any questions please email us at billing@novaface.com

How to upload photos?

Please go to Pictures page, browse for a picture on your local computer and click Submit. Your pictures must meet the following requirements: The size of uploaded pictures should not exceed 2MB. Only the jpg/jpeg, gif and png formats are supported. Note: We will delete photos that include nudity; drawings and paintings; famous people; baby/childhood photos.
Only Platinum members can upload pictures.

How my photos will be displayed on the website?

The main photo will be shown at all pages of this website, including the main page. The public photos will be shown at your profile page.

How do I add users to favorites?

Please click add to favorites link on the selected member profile page.
