Education in the USA

Invest in your children's future!

Far East Business can help realize your dream of studying abroad!

It is widely regarded that studying abroad is several times more effective than even advanced classes at home. Foreign schools offer a unique combination of the latest teaching methods and decades-old experience; tout the most professional educators and rare traditions; and are privy to the most innovative technology and the most comfortable amenities.

Excursions, sports training and game-day events, and the ability to choose programs and classes based on individual interests all make studying abroad entertaining and engaging.

 Given the amount of summer schools, language learning courses, colleges, universities, and other educational institutions, it is difficult to choose not only the finest institution, but the one that best fits your needs. It is a careful, time-intensive process that requires precision, resourcefulness, and commitment.

 The educational consulting company Far East Business has been attending to all matters related to schooling in the United States. One of the company’s advantages is that our agents have emigrated from Russia to receive their higher education in the US, meaning they themselves have gone through this difficult process before.

 We will be happy to share with you the invaluable information we have gathered over the years. We understand that each student is unique, and we are committed to providing individualized service to answer all questions regarding the application process. We make it our priority to deliver you the results you seek. Far East Business is your guide to getting your children quality education in America.

 We offer these service:

 -Help in getting your children into the finest American boarding schools;

 -Helping students attend the best American universities in order to receive a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree;

 -Overseeing students throughout their studies and offering consulting services;

 -Help in selecting summer or year-long language learning programs for children and students;

 -Meeting and dropping off students at a New York City airport and helping them move into their dorms;

 -Securing a cell phone and helping open a bank account.

Our company does not limit our services at simply helping fill out documents. We are also in constant engagement with the educational institution that your child wants to attend, and consult on all related services.

We work very closely with every pupil to help each one make the best decisions, while making the application process quick and easy.
