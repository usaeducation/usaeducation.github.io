Invest in your children's future!

Summer and year-round language courses in the U.S.

Far East Business - is your guide to getting your children quality education in America!

In the US, there is a rich selection of summer language programs for children and young adults. Summer courses in America can be divided into two categories: traditional English courses, offered by language schools; or language and academic courses, put together by private boarding schoolsor other companies. Only English courses in the US can guarantee full immersion into the language environment. The vocabulary grows in the shortest time possible, eliminating the language barrier. Generally, learning English in America gives better-than-expected results.

Intenational summer school

International Summer Program   Princeton, NJ. English Language Program includes both academic sessions and cultural programs. Our school has students from different countries: from Japan to Europe and the USA. In addition to the educational component, students learn to be independent, take responsibility for their actions, show respect and honesty.

Studying English in the US has many advantages. Because students will be learning literally at all times during their stay, it allows them to get acquainted with America’s geography and regional peculiarities. Itgoes hand-in-hand with learning other subjects, such asUnited States history. Of course, learning the language comes with fascinating excursions, visits to museums and exhibitions, iconic landmarks; in other words, the ability to touch the culture of America’s past and present.

"Far East Business" offers access to a wide range of summer and year-round programs for children, students and professionals. Contact us, and we will find a program that caters to your interests and wishes.
